Checking Saffron Authenticity

Because Saffron (Kesar) is high-value, it is frequently blended with cheap materials or dyed red. At Ridwan DryFruits, we encourage our clients to test their saffron. Here is how pure Kashmiri Saffron (Mongra) behaves compared to adulterated alternatives.

1. The Color Release Speed

Fake saffron is coated in artificial water-soluble red dyes that bleed into the water instantly. Pure saffron threads take time to release crocin. The yellow tint will start spreading slowly over 5 to 15 minutes, eventually yielding a golden, sunshine-yellow liquid.

2. The Water Float Test

Pure Kashmiri Mongra threads are light and strong. When dropped in cold or warm water, they remain floating near the surface. Fake or weighted threads (coated in sugar syrup or mineral oil) sink quickly to the bottom of the glass.

3. Thread Quality After Soaking

Remove a thread from the water after 20 minutes. A pure thread remains intact, holding its bright crimson shade. Fake threads are fragile, disintegrate instantly, and lose their red color completely, leaving a pale or white string.

4. Smell vs Taste Rule

Pure saffron has a sweet, honey-like aroma, but it tastes strictly bitter. If the saffron tastes sweet, it has been treated with honey, sugar syrup, or glycerine to artificially increase its weight.

Try the Water Test Simulation

Simulate dropping saffron threads in warm water and observe the difference.

Our Legacy and Location

For generations, the Pulwama and Pampore regions in Kashmir have been home to some of the richest agricultural treasures on Earth. Known for its perfect volcanic soils and chilly weather, this area produces dry fruits that are unmatched in aroma, taste, and oil concentrations.

Ridwan DryFruits (led by Ridwan Sir) is located at Pandit Traders Circle Road, Tahab Crossing in Pulwama, Jammu & Kashmir. What began as a simple service providing direct dry fruits to visitors has evolved into a dedicated store sending premium dry fruits all over India.

Our business philosophy is simple: keep packaging minimal and quality high. We handpick, sort, shell, and pack all walnuts and almonds locally. Our saffron undergoes rigorous dry-vial packing to keep external moisture completely sealed away.
